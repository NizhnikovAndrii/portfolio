One day, a lawyer approached me with a request to create a logo for his agency. There were no specific requirements. He simply left it up to my discretion. However, his professional style inspired me with an idea.

I suggested designing a logo that would reflect his image. He was always dressed in a shirt and tie, and I envisioned incorporating this style as the foundational element of his logo. For example, I used the concept of a white collar and a tie that he often adjusted, which became the basis for the logo.

Another time, the owner of a private winery in Georgia approached me. He shared a legend about a grape cluster that emerged from a wooden wheel that had fallen off and remained in the shade under a tree by the road for a long time. All he desired was a logo in the form of a crest to represent this legend.

I illustrated his legend and also created label designs for the wine bottles in accordance with this concept. I hope these improvements have enhanced your text. If you have any further questions or need additional assistance, please let me know.

This logo represents a dynamic energy company, ETT (Electric Technology and Technologies). The client's vision was to convey strength and power, capturing the essence of energy itself.

Inspired by the elegance of Ohm's Law, a fundamental principle in physics, I crafted a captivating design. Ohm's Law illuminates the intricate interplay between electromotive force, current flow, and conductor resistance.

In this logo, the symbol Ω, denoting resistance, is ingeniously transformed into two interwoven letter T's. Symbolizing the untamed energy flowing through the system, the letter E gracefully curves, embodying the force of current restrained by the resistance it encounters.

With this artistic fusion of symbolism and science, the ETT logo becomes a captivating embodiment of power, vitality, and technological prowess, reflecting the dynamic spirit of the energy industry.

This logo is designed for a coffee shop. The client wanted it to be distinct from the typical coffee cup imagery and instead sought something unconventional and youthful.

I proposed positioning the name as if it were shattered granite tiles, with a coffee cart in the background where the delicious brew is prepared.

This approach creates a memorable logo that captures attention and exudes a youthful atmosphere. It stands out from traditional coffee shop logos, embodying the boldness and originality of the establishment.
