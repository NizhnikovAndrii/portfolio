In today's fast-paced modern life, time is often our most precious commodity. We usually feel like we're running out of time. The Night Tale App will ease up the care for what matters most to you: your children.

No longer do you need to worry about spending hours choosing a bedtime story. Our app solves this problem in an instant. Just press play, and you'll find in a perfect fairy tale in a moment.

This is an application that will create a fairy tale for children with the help of artificial intelligence. The intellect does everything for you.

The AI generates images and text for fairy tales on request.

I wanted to create a simple user-friendly and easy interface. Fairy tales are divided into six main categories.

Once a category is chosen, the fairy tale begins!

Just click play and the fairy tale will be read by a pleasant voice. If a child does not want to listen to the AI voice, mom or dad can read it themselves, as the text generated is easy to read.

I have created a logo for this app, which will also serve as a easy-to-recognise favicon
