PATCH 2023

  • Role
  • Creator
  • Product
  • Visual design
  • Documentation

Embarking on the design of a mobile application tailored for American students, focusing on university search and selection, has been an exhilarating journey in which I played a pivotal role. The primary objective was to craft an interface that is user-friendly and aligns seamlessly with the brand standards.

Throughout the project, I not only took into consideration but also embraced the color scheme of the brand, ensuring the application exudes recognition and consistency with the corporate style. This allows users to easily associate the app with reliability and quality.

One of the key components of the application is the screen dedicated to university selection. My goal was to simplify this process to the utmost, allowing any student to effortlessly and swiftly access information about various institutions. I concentrated on a minimalist design, emphasizing core elements and providing an intuitive navigation experience.

As a result, my work culminated in not only a functional application but also a visually appealing tool for exploring educational institutions. I aspire for my design not only to meet the expectations of clients but also to inspire students towards a successful educational journey, encouraging them to make informed choices about their academic paths.
